Coding Resources For Beginners

Scratch

Scratch is a website, where you can make amazing projects, by just using blocks of code! Just make an account, and start your thrilling adventure, in Scratch!

W3schools

W3schools is an html editor, with tutorials if you a beginner, and a place for you to make your own code! You don't need an account, so you can just have fun!

Thunkable

Thunkable is an app, to make apps on your phone. It provides tutorials, and in Thunkable you use coding blocks, and buttons to make your wonderful app!

Khan Academy

In Khan Academy the possiblities are limitless. You get to have tutorials, make websites, and even use it for school related studies! Now that's amazing!
